Projects

Elon University Website

August, 2015

Elon University is a small, private university in Elon, North Carolina. Elon's student newspaper, The Pendulum, came to us looking for a refresh. Their organization continued to use the print first method and neglected to focus much time on their website. With a change in organizational workflows, they decided to put their website at the front and came to us at SNworks with hopes of a much more engaging website. I was the project manager for this client and worked with them through every step of the process to ensure their new site was what they had in mind.

Daily Cardinal Website

January, 2016

University of Wisconsin - Madison's student publication, The Daily Cardinal, came to us over the summer of 2015 to create a new website for them. The CMS (Content Management System) they were using was outdated and made their workflow unnecessarily hard. They also were looking for a new website as they transitioned from printing everyday to printing just twice a week. This was forcing them to put more focus on their digital content.
